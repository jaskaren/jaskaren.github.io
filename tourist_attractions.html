Paris

Tourist Attractions

There are many attractions in Paris. Besides some of the world's most famous musuems, its has a vibrant historic city centre, a beautiful riverscape, an extensive range of historic monuments, including cathedrals, chapels and palaces, plus one of the most famous nightlife scenes in the world.

  • Paris musuems:The Louvre is one of the world's great art galleries, with masterpieces such as the Mona Lisa and works by almost all the greatest painters.
  • The Eiffel Tower:You can take the lift to the top, for a spectacular panorama of Paris.
  • Disneyland Paris:Disneyland Paris is more than just a theme park with spectacular rides. It’s a resort with hotels, shopping and golf among its great variety of activities
  • Champs-Elysées:The tree-lined Avenue des Champs-Elysees is Paris’s most famous street and has even been described as the most beautiful avenue in the world.
  • Palace of Versailles:The Palace of Versailles ceased being a royal residence during the French Revolution and today houses a museum of French history.
